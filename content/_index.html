About Rush

Stop At Nothing

Stop At Nothing [Rush] is a NA SEA fight-focused WvW guild in Guild Wars 2.

We primarily run WvW squad in SEA time (9PM - 11PM GMT+8 / 1PM - 3PM GMT) in weekends and sometimes in weekdays. Currently we are on {{% gw2-current-world %}} world.
